Features

Everything you need to study better

GYAN brings together the tools that actually help you build consistent study habits and reach your goals.

⏱

Focus Timer

Per-subject Pomodoro timer with ambient sounds (rain, ocean, forest, cafe). Tracks time even when minimized with a lock-screen overlay.

🔥

Daily Streaks

Build and maintain study streaks to stay consistent. Visual weekly calendar shows your progress and keeps you motivated.

🤖

AI Quiz Generator

Generate quizzes from any subject using AI. Test your knowledge with auto-generated questions and instant grading.

📄

Answer Sheet Scanner

Upload and scan answer sheets for instant AI analysis. Get detailed feedback on your performance and areas to improve.

👥

Study Groups

Join or create study groups with friends. Collaborate, share progress, and keep each other accountable.

📊

Statistics & Insights

Detailed analytics on your study patterns. Track total hours, sessions per subject, and weekly trends at a glance.
